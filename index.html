What We Do

From B2B wholesale to e-commerce retail, we operate across the full spectrum of the travel goods market.

We supply hard-side and soft-side luggage and travel bags to retailers and corporate buyers across the UK and internationally. Whether you're looking to stock a single brand or build a broader travel goods range, we offer the flexibility, stock and support to make it straightforward.

We hold licensing agreements with globally recognised travel brands, giving our retail partners access to premium, market-ready product ranges. Our in-house design and production capability means we manage the full product lifecycle — from concept and specification through to delivery-ready stock.

Through our e-commerce platform Go Places (goplaces.co.uk), we retail a wide selection of leading luggage brands direct to consumers — including Samsonite, American Tourister and Delsey. Acquired in 2017, Go Places is one of the UK's longest-running online luggage destinations, rated 4.9 on Trustpilot.

Who We Are

A family business at the heart of UK travel goods.

St.C Travel Goods has been at the heart of the UK travel goods industry since 2002. What started as a specialist distributor has grown into a full-service travel goods business, combining wholesale distribution, licensed brand management and one of the UK's longest-established luggage e-commerce operations.

We're a family-run business based from out head office and distribution warehouse in West Sussex — small enough to be genuinely responsive, experienced enough to work at scale. Our team has decades of combined expertise across product design, wholesale, retail partnerships and e-commerce. We know this industry inside out, and we're proud of the long-term relationships we've built with retailers, buyers and partners across the UK and beyond.

Named after St. Christopher — the patron saint of travel — it's a fitting home for everything we do.
